Activities

Guided Bee and Garden Tour

During this 2 hour tour we will walk through the farm and visit both the stingless bee hives and the "killer bees" that aren't very killer at all. Price: $16 per person.

Unguided Garden Walk

You can also come for a casual walk through the garden. You will be able to visit the stingless bee hives and enjoy the tranquility surrounding the farm. Entrance fee: $8.

Honey and Wine Tasting

Our bee hives produce the most delicious honey we have ever tasted, and with it we have made equally amazing honey mead. Come and try it out. Price: $20 per person.
