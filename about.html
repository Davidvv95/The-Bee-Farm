About Us

What is The Bee Farm?

The Bee Farm is a bee sanctuary on the slopes of the Barva volcano, where you will find dozens of species of bees pollinating the countless flowers all around the farm. They also help to keep our organic vegetable plot thriving throughout the year.

Mision

Our mission is to preserve a garden specially designated for bees to thrive. We aim to educate on the importance of bees; the value they bring to our lives and the planet.

Vision

To be the national benchmark on efforts for the preservation of bees and their ideal habitat
